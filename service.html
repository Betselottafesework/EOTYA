Services of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Youth Association (☦️):

The Ethiopian Orthodox Association offers a wide range of services that cater to the spiritual, cultural, and social needs of its community. With a focus on both faith and culture, the association ensures that all members have the resources and support they need to grow spiritually, connect with others, and preserve the rich heritage of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church. Below are the key services provided:

1. Spiritual Services
- Regular Worship Services
- Sacraments: Baptism, Holy Communion, Confession, Marriage, Anointing of the Sick
- Spiritual Counseling
- Bible Studies
- Pilgrimages

2. Cultural and Educational Services
- Cultural Events
- Language Classes (Ge'ez & Amharic)
- Workshops & Seminars
- Youth Programs

3. Community Support Services
- Charity & Outreach
- Crisis Support
- Youth Mentoring

4. Social Services
- Social Gatherings & Fellowship
- Elderly Assistance
- Marriage & Family Counseling

5. Ethiopian Orthodox Heritage Preservation
- Historical Preservation Projects
- Church Renovation & Preservation
- Cultural Exhibitions

6. Advocacy & Representation
- Cultural Advocacy
- Interfaith Dialogue

7. Online Resources & Digital Engagement
- Online Services & Livestreaming
- E-learning Opportunities
