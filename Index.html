Introduction:

Welcome to the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Youth Association (EOTYA), a vibrant community dedicated to empowering the youth of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church. Founded by Betselot Tafesework, EOTYA serves as a platform for young people to connect, grow, and deepen their faith while embracing the rich cultural heritage of Ethiopia.
